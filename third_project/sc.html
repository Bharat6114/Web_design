Strength & Conditioning

Strength & Conditioning (S&C) is recognised, worldwide, from amateur through to elite level sport as paramount importance to the ongoing physical development of athletes who compete in all different forms of sport.
Contrary to popular belief, S&C is not just lifting weights! In order to assist the athletes in coping with the unique physiological loads and demands of their sport. We design periodised programmes around your sporting calendar to develop key attributes such as movement mechanics, flexibility, strength, speed and agility, balance and co-ordination and rehab; resulting in reduced injury risks and increased performance.
